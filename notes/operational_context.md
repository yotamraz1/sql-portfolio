# Operational Context

These queries were written as part of day-to-day operational work on a live automated trading platform running 24/7 across multiple exchanges.

## What the analytics supported

- **Execution monitoring** — tracking whether trades were filling correctly, identifying anomalies in fill/reject behavior
- **Fee optimization** — understanding which instruments had favorable fee-to-profit ratios at scale
- **Machine health** — detecting if specific execution nodes were drifting from expected behavior
- **Workflow validation** — confirming that automated workflows were completing as expected
- **Daily performance tracking** — catching degradation early before it compounds

## My role

My primary involvement was on the operational and analytical side — writing and running queries to monitor system behavior, identify anomalies, and support data-driven decisions in a live production environment.

Core infrastructure and execution systems were primarily developed by my technical co-founder.
